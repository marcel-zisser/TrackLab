About

Who I Am

Hi, my name is Marcel, and I’m currently pursuing my master’s degree at Graz University of Technology. As part of my thesis project, I wanted to combine two things I deeply enjoy: working with data and my passion for Formula 1.

Why This Project

Formula 1 has always fascinated me — not just the thrill of racing, but also the incredible depth of data and engineering behind every lap, corner, and decision. Modern races generate vast amounts of telemetry data, and I wanted to create a tool that makes this information accessible, engaging, and insightful for fans and researchers alike.

For Enthusiasts,
By an Enthusiast

At its core, this project is built for people like me — F1 fans who love to go beyond the broadcast and uncover the numbers driving the sport. I hope this tool helps you gain new insights, spark discussions, and deepen your appreciation for the data-driven world of Formula 1.
